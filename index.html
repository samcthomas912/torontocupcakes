DELIVERY POLICY

Locations

Cupcake delivery Toronto and GTA (approximately 80km surrounding Toronto). This includes Hamilton, Barrie, Markham, Scarborough, Vaughn, Ajax, and Pickering, to name a few.

Times

Our cupcake delivery service is available 7 days a week. Most deliveries can be made on the same day you order. If you need a delivery to a location that is outside of downtown Toronto we require notice at least the night before. Morning deliveries are typically for M** postal codes.

Fees

Delivery fee is based on the delivery location postal code and in special cases, time of day. Order three dozen or more to downtown Toronto and get free delivery or greatly reduced for other locations.
